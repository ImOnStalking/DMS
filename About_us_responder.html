About Us

Learn more about Disaster Supply Matching and our mission to connect disaster-affected communities with the supplies they need.

Who We Are

Disaster Supply Matching is a platform dedicated to connecting disaster-affected communities with the right supplies at the right time. Our Mission

To ensure every donation reaches those who need it most, reducing waste and improving emergency response through efficient matching of supplies.

Our Vision

We envision a world where communities in crisis receive timely and accurate support, and where every act of generosity creates a meaningful impact.

Our Values

Compassion

We care about the well-being of those affected by disasters.

Transparency

Every action and donation is tracked to ensure accountability.

Efficiency

We prioritize speed and accuracy in delivering supplies.

Collaboration

We work with donors, volunteers, and local responders to maximize impact.

Innovation

We use technology to improve disaster response and resource distribution.

Our History

Founded in 2018, Disaster Supply Matching began as a small community initiative to provide emergency aid during typhoons and earthquakes. Over the years, we have expanded nationwide, developing a platform that connects donors, volunteers, and NGOs to communities in need with speed and efficiency.

Our Team

Our team consists of disaster response specialists, logistics coordinators, volunteers, and technology experts. We work together to ensure supplies are delivered accurately and efficiently, keeping the needs of affected communities at the center of all operations.

Our Partners

We collaborate with NGOs, local governments, corporations, and community organizations to enhance the reach and effectiveness of our operations. These partnerships allow us to verify needs, distribute supplies responsibly, and maintain transparency in every step of the process.

Our Impact

Since our founding, Disaster Supply Matching has helped thousands of families and communities receive essential supplies during emergencies. Through efficient coordination and technology-driven solutions, we continue to improve disaster response and make a meaningful difference in the lives of those affected.

How We Work

We gather real-time data on supply needs from affected communities and partner organizations. Our platform matches these needs with available resources, ensuring that donations reach the right place at the right time. By streamlining disaster response, we reduce waste and maximize impact.
