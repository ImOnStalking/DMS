FIRE IN COTABATO

A fire recently affected a residential area in Cotabato, leaving several families displaced and in need of immediate support. Many lost their homes and basic necessities, making daily survival difficult. We are asking for donations to help provide food, clothing, and essential supplies for the affected families.
