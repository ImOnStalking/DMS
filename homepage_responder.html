About Us

Disaster Supply Matching is a platform dedicated to streamlining the distribution of essential supplies during natural disasters and emergencies. We bridge the gap between generous donors and frontline responders to ensure aid reaches those who need it quickly and efficiently.

Key Features

Quick Supply Requests

Submit urgent requests for supplies with priority settings to ensure timely delivery to affected areas.

Real-Time Coordination

Coordinate with donors and admins in real-time to match supplies with needs efficiently.

Delivery Tracking

Monitor delivery statuses and logistics to ensure supplies reach responders on time.
