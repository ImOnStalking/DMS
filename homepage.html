About Us

Disaster Supply Matching is a platform dedicated to streamlining the distribution of essential supplies during natural disasters and emergencies. We bridge the gap between generous donors and frontline responders to ensure aid reaches those who need it quickly and efficiently.

Key Features

Supply Requests

Responders can submit detailed requests for supplies needed in disaster zones, specifying quantities and priorities.

Donations

Donors can easily contribute supplies or funds, with real-time tracking of their impact on relief efforts.

Inventory Management

Comprehensive tracking of available supplies, ensuring optimal allocation and reducing waste during crises.

Disaster Supply Matching

Helping communities recover faster through efficient supply distribution.
